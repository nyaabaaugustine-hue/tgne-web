Print Technology

Our Printing Methods

We use the right technique for your garment, design and quantity to deliver the best possible result.

🖶
Screen Printing
The gold standard for bold, vibrant graphics on bulk orders. Inks are pressed through a mesh screen for crisp, durable results that last years.
Best for 12+ pieces
Bold, vivid colours
Most durable method
Great for uniforms
🌟
Sublimation Printing
Heat-transfer dye fuses directly into the fabric fibre. Perfect for full-colour, all-over prints and sports jerseys with stunning photographic quality.
Full all-over coverage
Photographic quality
Ideal for sportswear
Fade-resistant

## BRAND VOICE & TONE

### Voice Characteristics
- Professional yet approachable
- Clear and concise
- Knowledgeable without being condescending
- Solution-focused
- Confident and trustworthy

### Tone Guidelines

**When writing for TGNE Solutions:**
✅ DO:
- Use active voice
- Be direct and clear
- Focus on benefits
- Use "we" and "you"
- Show expertise
- Be encouraging

❌ DON'T:
- Use jargon unnecessarily
- Be overly technical
- Make empty promises
- Use corporate speak
- Be pushy or salesy
- Overcomplicate

### Example Phrases

**Good:**
- "Let's transform your business together"
- "We make technology work for you"
- "Your success is our priority"
- "Expert solutions, personal service"

**Avoid:**
- "Leverage synergies to optimize"
- "Best-in-class solutions"
- "Revolutionary game-changer"
- "One-stop-shop for all your needs"

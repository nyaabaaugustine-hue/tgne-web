## FAQ

Q: How many design concepts will I receive?
A: Typically 2-3 initial concepts, with unlimited revisions on your chosen design.

Q: What file formats will I receive?
A: All source files (AI, PSD) plus print-ready PDFs and web-optimized formats (PNG, JPG, SVG).

Q: How long does a design project take?
A: Logo design: 1-2 weeks. Full brand identity: 3-4 weeks. Marketing materials: 1-2 weeks.

Q: Can you match my existing brand colors?
A: Absolutely! We can work within your existing brand guidelines or help refresh them.

Q: Do you provide printing services?
A: Yes! We can handle both design and printing, or provide print-ready files for your printer.

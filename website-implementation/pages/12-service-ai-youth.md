## PROGRAM OVERVIEW

Content:
The future belongs to those who understand technology. Our AI for Youth Development program introduces students to artificial intelligence through hands-on, age-appropriate activities that spark curiosity, build critical thinking skills, and prepare them for tomorrow's opportunities. From coding basics to ethical AI use, we make learning about AI fun and accessible.

## FAQ

Q: Does my child need prior coding experience?
A: No! Our programs are designed for beginners. We start with the basics.

Q: What equipment does my child need?
A: For in-person programs, we provide everything. For online programs, just a computer with internet.

Q: How many students per class?
A: Maximum 12 students to ensure personalized attention.

Q: Will my child learn actual programming?
A: Yes! Age-appropriate programming is integrated throughout all programs.

Q: Is this just for kids interested in technology?
A: No! AI literacy is important for all students, regardless of career interests.

Q: Can my child join mid-program?
A: It depends on the program and availability. Contact us to discuss options.

Q: Do you offer scholarships?
A: Yes, limited scholarships available based on need. Contact us for information.

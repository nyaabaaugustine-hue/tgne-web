## WHAT IS CNC PRODUCTION

Content:
CNC (Computer Numerical Control) machining is an advanced manufacturing process that uses computerized controls to operate machine tools with exceptional precision. We transform your digital designs into physical products with accuracy measured in thousandths of an inch.

## FAQ

Q: What file formats do you accept?
A: DXF, DWG, STL, STEP, IGES. We can also work from PDF drawings or create CAD files for you.

Q: How long does CNC production take?
A: Simple parts: 2-3 days. Complex parts: 5-7 days. Production runs: 1-2 weeks.

Q: What's your minimum order quantity?
A: No minimum! We handle single prototypes to production runs of 1000+ pieces.

Q: Can you work from hand sketches?
A: Yes! We can create CAD files from your sketches or rough drawings.

Q: Do you offer finishing services?
A: Yes, including sanding, painting, polishing, and assembly.

Q: What tolerances can you achieve?
A: Standard: ±0.005". Precision: ±0.001" for critical dimensions.

## FAQ SECTION

Heading: Frequently Asked Questions

### Question 1
Q: How long does it take to build a website?
A: Typically 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Simple sites can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while complex e-commerce sites may take 8-12 weeks.

### Question 2
Q: Will my website be mobile-friendly?
A: Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.

### Question 3
Q: Can I update the website myself?
A: Yes! We build websites on WordPress, an easy-to-use platform. We also provide training so you can make updates confidently.

### Question 4
Q: Do you provide hosting?
A: We can recommend reliable hosting providers and assist with setup, or work with your existing hosting.

### Question 5
Q: What if I need changes after launch?
A: We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages. Minor updates are often included in your support plan.

### Question 6
Q: Will my website be SEO-friendly?
A: Yes! We implement SEO best practices including proper structure, meta tags, fast loading, and mobile optimization.
